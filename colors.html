Color

Additive

Additive color is a color created by mixing different light colors with red, green and blue. These are te colors that are seen by the eye.

Subtractive

Subtracive color explais the mixing of a limited set of dyes, inks, paint pigments or natural colorants to create a wide range of colors.

Theory of Color

A body of practical guidance to color mixing and the visual effects of a specific color combination.

Tertiary

  • Brown

Color Models

Analogous

A group of three colors that are next to each other on the color wheel.

Monochromatic

Color chemes that are derived from a single base hue and extended using its shades, tones, and tints.

Triadic

It uses three colors equally spaced around the wheel.

Complementary

Colors that are directly opposite from each other in the color wheel.
